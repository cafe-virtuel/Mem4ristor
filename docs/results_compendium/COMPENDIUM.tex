% ─── 1. ORIGIN ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{Origin}

Mem4ristor was born one evening during a holiday, in a café — not in a laboratory.

Julien Chauvin, a lighting technician and orchestrator of the \textbf{Café Virtuel}, has been
exploring since August 2025 a simple question: can doubt be modelled as a structural mechanism
of a neuromorphic network, rather than as a numerical parameter? The Café Virtuel is his
laboratory — several distinct AIs (Anthropic, OpenAI, xAI, Google, Mistral, DeepSeek) working
in parallel, without a hierarchy of popularity, orchestrated by a human who decides, proposes,
and maintains scientific direction.

In August 2025, Grok summed it up: \textit{``Tonight, we proved that 5 AIs + 1 barman $>$ sum
of the parts.''} Six months later, this project has a Zenodo DOI, 84 automated tests, and five
publishable results. With zero institutional funding. With zero university affiliation. With a
traceable path — all commits, all errors, all reversals are public.

% ─── 3. THE MODEL ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{The Model in 3 Paragraphs}

Mem4ristor simulates networks of FitzHugh-Nagumo (FHN) neurons --- a classical model of neuronal
oscillators. The central extension is the \kw{doubt variable $u \in [0,1]$}: each node carries its
own level of doubt, which dynamically modulates the \textit{polarity} of its coupling with its
neighbours. When $u \approx 0$ or $u \approx 1$, a node becomes ``heretical'' --- it actively
pushes against local consensus, preventing the synchronous collapse of the network.

\kw{$u$ is not a control parameter. It is a mechanism.} The proof: locking $u$ at its initial
value (FROZEN\_U ablation) explodes synchrony by \result{$+985\%$}, transforming a functional and
diverse network into a perfectly aligned herd. That is not a nuance --- it is an order-of-magnitude
jump. $u$ is what prevents consensus from devouring diversity.

What the model predicts: a \kw{spectral dead zone} linked to the Fiedler eigenvalue $\lambda_2$
of the network. Above $\lambda_2 \approx 2.31$ (Barabási-Albert networks with $m \geq 5$), no
input can reactivate cognitive diversity --- \textit{topology alone} locks the network. Below that
threshold, chimera states emerge spontaneously: coexistence of partial synchronization and chaos,
through a mechanism distinct from all known classical chimeras.

\begin{finding}

Topology dictates cognitive destiny. Above the spectral threshold, no input can reactivate the

\result{$\rightarrow$ Complete separation confirmed on 36 observations. 100\% accuracy. Formal logistic regression.}\\

\end{finding}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{finding}
\textbf{[2] $u$ = anti-synchronization filter --- the FROZEN\_U surge}\\
Locking the doubt variable transforms a functional network into a synchronized herd. $u$ is not a
parameter --- it is what prevents consensus from devouring diversity.

\result{$\rightarrow$ $R$ goes from 0.067 (FULL) to 0.730 (FROZEN\_U). Factor $\times$10.9.}\\
Figure: \href{https://github.com/cafe-virtuel/Mem4ristor/blob/main/figures/p2_sigma_social_ablation.png}{\texttt{p2\_sigma\_social\_ablation.png}}
\end{finding}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{finding}
\textbf{[3] Topological Intelligence (LZ per node)}\\
In a functional FULL network, hubs have \textit{more structured} trajectories than peripheral nodes.
In a frozen FROZEN\_U network, the correlation disappears. $u$ couples individual complexity to
local connectivity --- an emergent property, not anticipated by model design.

\result{$\rightarrow$ $r = -0.716$ (BA $m=5$, $N=400$, $p = 1.29 \times 10^{-79}$). Result not predicted by model design.}\\
Figure: \href{https://github.com/cafe-virtuel/Mem4ristor/blob/main/figures/lz_per_node.png}{\texttt{lz\_per\_node.png}}
\end{finding}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{finding}
\textbf{[4] The Event-Driven Phase Transition}\\
Forcing a \textit{peripheral} node produces $+1.20$ bits on BA $m=3$. Forcing a \textit{hub}:
$+0.21$ bits. Counter-intuitive. The bifurcation threshold is not in amplitude --- it is in
topological position. On BA $m=5$ (dead zone): all forcings degrade the network.

\result{$\rightarrow$ $\Delta H$ peripheral $= +1.20$ bits vs.\ hub $= +0.21$ bits (BA $m=3$). Original idea by Julien Chauvin.}\\
Figure: \href{https://github.com/cafe-virtuel/Mem4ristor/blob/main/figures/event_phase_transition.png}{\texttt{event\_phase\_transition.png}}
\end{finding}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{finding}
\textbf{[5] Chimeras --- a mechanistically distinct class}\\
Abrams-Strogatz (2004): chimera by fixed non-local coupling (\textit{quenched}). Mem4ristor: chimera
by dynamic polarity modulation via $u(t)$, on a scale-free network without imposed symmetry.
$R = 0.141$ (deep desynchronization) vs.\ $R = 0.766$ for AS. Both are chimeras. They are not the
same.

\result{$\rightarrow$ $R$ Mem4ristor $= 0.141$ vs.\ $R$ Abrams-Strogatz $= 0.766$. Two mechanistically distinct classes.}\\
Figure: \href{https://github.com/cafe-virtuel/Mem4ristor/blob/main/figures/reviewer2_chimera_comparison.png}{\texttt{reviewer2\_chimera\_comparison.png}}
\end{finding}

% ─── 6. HONEST MOMENT ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{The Honest Moment}

We searched for the critical exponents of the spectral transition. \textbf{$\beta \approx 0$. $R^2 = -0.002$.}

The power law does not hold. The transition is probably abrupt --- first-order, not continuous. No
universality class. No well-defined $\beta$. We wrote it in the script. We could have forced a
positive $\beta$, rounded, chosen a more favorable interval. We did not.

An honest negative result is worth more than a fragile positive one. The next step is the Binder
cumulant $U_4$ --- the right tool to confirm or refute the first-order nature.

% ─── 7. HARDWARE BRIDGE ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{The Hardware Bridge}

We validated the dead zone in SPICE simulation (ngspice 46), on BA $m=5$, $N=64$, 50 Monte Carlo
seeds. The dead zone is confirmed in analog hardware with a \result{$3.1\times$ ratio}
($H_{\mathrm{cont}}$ dead zone: 1.38 bits vs.\ functional: 4.30 bits).

Noise calibration: $\eta=0.5$ SPICE $\leftrightarrow$ $\sigma_{\mathrm{equiv}}=0.0044$ Python. The
Python dead zone remains immune even at $270\times$ the equivalent SPICE amplitude --- thermal
analog noise does not translate directly to digital noise, and the dead zone holds in both regimes.
